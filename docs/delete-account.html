Delete Your PhotoLynk Account

Request permanent deletion of your StealthCloud account and all associated data.

What Gets Deleted

  • Account — Your email and authentication credentials
  • Photos & Videos — All encrypted backups on StealthCloud
  • Encryption Keys — Your encrypted file manifests
  • Device Data — All device associations and sync history

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All data will be deleted within 30 days.

Request Account Deletion

To verify your identity and delete your account, please email us with the following information:

Required information:

  • Email — The email address used for your PhotoLynk account
  • Password — Your account password (for verification)
  • Device UUID — Found in PhotoLynk app: Settings → About → Device UUID
Request Account Deletion

No email app? Send an email manually to:

support@stealthlynk.io

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include your email, password, and Device UUID in the body.

Timeline

We will verify your identity and process your deletion request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once complete.
